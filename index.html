About Us

Rapix Connect was founded by a pharmacist with first-hand experience managing medication shortages. Having faced the daily challenges and frustrations of out-of-stock queries, we recognised that the current communication setup between GP surgeries and community pharmacies is no longer fit for purpose in 2025. Staff at GP surgeries are still spending valuable time calling pharmacy after pharmacy to locate medicines in short supply. Surely, there had to be a better way! The solution? Rapix Connect.

At Rapix Connect, our mission is simple: to bridge the communication gap between GP surgeries and community pharmacies, ensuring patients get vital medications quickly without unnecessary delays.

We believe in real-time solutions for real-world problems.

  • Benefits for patients

    Sadly there has been recent instances of patient harm and death due to out of stock medication. The Rapix connect platform will help patients get the crucial medication they require without the frustrating delays.

  • Benefits for healthcare staff

    Medication shortages queries can be very stressful for staff. Spend less time on the phone dealing with shortages queries, and more time with your patients.

  • Environmental benefits

    Patients often drive from pharmacy to pharmacy to locate medication. The Rapix connect platform will help minimise journeys via our digital solution.

How does it work

GP to pharmacy- This allows the GP staff to locate via local pharmacies, medication which has no straight forward alternative.

Pharmacy to GP- This allows pharmacies to suggest an alternative to an out of stock medication.

Step 1: GP creates a new medication request

Simple interface - just enter details like 'Methylphenidate, XL 18mg, 28 tablets

Step 2: Area-Wide Broadcast

Request is securely sent to all local pharmacies simultaneously with one click.

Step 3:One-click reply

Pharmacies reply with one of four options, in-stock, can order, unavailable, suggest alternative .

Step 1: Search for GP surgery

Pharmacy simply types the name of GP surgery to send the message to. Say good-bye to long queues trying to call the GP surgery.

Step 2: Suggest alternative

Type medication which is out of stock, eg betamethasone 0.1% cream 100g. Then suggest alternative that is available, eg betamethasone 0.1% ointment 100g.

Step 3: One-click reply

GP staff reply to alternative pharmacy suggestions with either accept, or reject.
